Visit Dhaka

Dhaka is the capital city of Bangladesh.
With a rich history, diverse culture, bustling streets, and delicious food.

Top Three Things To Do In Dhaka

Delicious Street Food

Street food in Dhaka is a vibrant and diverse culinary scene, offering an array of delicious and affordable snacks and meals that reflect the city's rich cultural heritage.

Explore the Lalbagh Fort

Lalbagh Fort is an iconic historical monument that was built in the mid 17th century. It is a great place to explore if you are interested in history, architecture, and culture.

Visit the Ahsan Manzil

Ahsan Manzil, also known as the Pink Palace, is a stunning 19th century palace located on the banks of the Buriganga River. It was once the residence of the Nawab of Dhaka.
